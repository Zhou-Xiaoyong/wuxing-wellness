The five-element quiz is a great shortcut, but some people feel it misses the texture of who they are. If that's you, trust your own observation. Long before there were online quizzes, people read their type from everyday life — the season they loved, the way stress showed up, even the foods they craved. Here are five signals you can check without answering a single question.

This is a reflection tool, not medical advice. Use it to get curious, not to diagnose yourself.

## 1. The season you're born into — or feel most alive in

In five-element thinking, each element owns a season. People often feel most *themselves* in their element's time of year:

- Born in spring, or you bloom in spring? Likely **Wood**.
- Summer person through and through? Likely **Fire**.
- You love the calm of late summer and harvest? Likely **Earth**.
- Autumn is your reset button? Likely **Metal**.
- You come alive in winter stillness? Likely **Water**.

## 2. Your default stress reaction

Stress is the clearest tell. Notice what happens *first*, before you cope:

- **Wood** → irritation and a need to move or fix.
- **Fire** → scattered, talkative, or suddenly exhausted.
- **Earth** → worry, overthinking, and wanting to care for someone.
- **Metal** → withdrawal, crispness, and a desire to tidy up.
- **Water** → shutting down, hiding, and conserving.

## 3. What your body complains about

Traditional practice links each element to an organ system, so chronic patterns are telling. Tight neck and shoulders under pressure (Wood/liver), restless heart and poor sleep (Fire/heart), sluggish digestion when anxious (Earth/spleen), shallow breathing or dry skin in fall (Metal/lungs), cold lower back and low drive in winter (Water/kidneys). These are observations, not diagnoses.

## 4. The foods you crave

Cravings hint at what your system reaches for. Wood reaches for **sour (酸)** foods like lemon and pickles; Fire for cooling, watery foods; Earth for sweet, grounding starches; Metal for pungent, clarifying flavors; Water for warm, salty, slow-cooked broths. None of this is a rule — just a nudge.

## 5. Your social style

- **Wood** leads, competes, and delegates.
- **Fire** connects, performs, and energizes the room.
- **Earth** hosts, nurtures, and keeps everyone fed.
- **Metal** selects a few close friends and guards their time.
- **Water** observes, listens, and shares little until trusted.

## Quick self-check: which signals match you?

Check the line that fits you best in each pair.

| Statement | Feels like me |
| --- | --- |
| My stress shows up as irritation and a need to act | ☐ |
| My stress shows up as withdrawal or a need for quiet | ☐ |
| I'm restored by movement and fresh air | ☐ |
| I'm restored by deep solitude and sleep | ☐ |
| I crave routine, connection, and being needed | ☐ |
| I value order, quality, and a small inner circle | ☐ |

Whichever column you checked more often is your likely dominant element.
