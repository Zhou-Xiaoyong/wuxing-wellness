Most five-element quizzes ask how you *feel* and act. The body type quiz asks a different question: what does your *physical build* quietly suggest about your elemental mix? In traditional Chinese wellness, constitution (体质, tǐ zhì) is read from more than mood — bone structure, frame, skin, and even how you carry tension all point toward one element running a little louder than the others.

This is a reflection tool drawn from a two-thousand-year-old framework, not a medical or diagnostic assessment. Nothing here is medical advice. If you have real concerns about your body or health, a licensed clinician is the right person to ask.

## Why body type and personality overlap

The same energy that shapes a sharp temper can also shape a sharp chin. In five-element thinking, your dominant element tends to leave fingerprints on both temperament *and* physique:

- **Wood** builds tend to be taller, leaner, with defined tendons and a quick, springy gait.
- **Fire** builds often have a pointed or reddish complexion, bright eyes, and restless, expressive movement.
- **Earth** builds run sturdy and soft, with a calm, grounded posture and a rounder midsection.
- **Metal** builds tend toward neat, refined features, fair skin, and precise, contained gestures.
- **Water** builds often read as deeper, darker, with a reserved hold and remarkable stamina hidden under stillness.

None of these are boxes you're locked into. Most bodies borrow from two elements — a Fire-faced person with an Earth-strong frame is completely ordinary.

## How the body type quiz is scored

Like the personality version, the body type quiz groups questions by element — but instead of "how do you react," it asks "how are you built." You might rate how true each description feels:

- Narrow shoulders and a long neck? That leans **Wood**.
- Warm palms and a flushed face after heat? That leans **Fire**.
- A soft belly and a love of routine meals? That leans **Earth**.
- Dry skin in autumn and a neat, almost formal posture? That leans **Metal**.
- Cold hands and feet, dark circles, and deep reserves? That leans **Water**.

Your highest tally points to your constitutional tendency. The runner-up explains the contradictions you've always noticed in the mirror.

## Reading the result without overthinking it

A body type isn't a verdict. It's a hint about what your system quietly prefers:

- If Wood leads, your frame likes *movement* and dislikes being cramped.
- If Fire leads, your skin and circulation respond fast to heat and stress.
- If Earth leads, your digestion and energy swing with routine and comfort.
- If Metal leads, your skin, lungs, and boundaries appreciate order and clean air.
- If Water leads, your warmth and kidney-adjacent energy need protecting in cold.

> The useful part isn't the label. It's noticing that your body has been telling you its preferences all along — you just finally have a language for it.

## Quick self-check: which build feels most like you?

| Statement | Feels like me |

Circle the rows that landed. The cluster you circled is your likely dominant element — but trust the pattern, not a single trait.
