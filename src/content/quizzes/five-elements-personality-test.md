This short test is built from the five-element (五行) personality framework used in traditional Chinese
practice. Each statement belongs to one element. Rate how strongly it feels like you, and your
highest-scoring element is your dominant type.

The questions load on the page above — answer all of them, then click **See my element**. No account, no
email, nothing stored.
