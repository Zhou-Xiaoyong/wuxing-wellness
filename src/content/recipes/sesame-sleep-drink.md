Black sesame is a traditional Water/kidney food in Chinese food therapy — warm, dark and "nourishing." Paired with walnut, it makes a soothing last cup before bed.

This is food, not medicine.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp black sesame paste (or ground toasted seeds)
- 1 tbsp walnut pieces
- 1 cup warm milk of choice
- Optional: few drops honey

## Method

1. Whisk sesame paste into warm milk until smooth.
2. Stir in walnut pieces.
3. Sip warm, slowly, as the last thing before bed.

## When to reach for it

Traditional practice pairs this with Water-time sleep signals: "wired but tired," waking 2–4 a.m., or cold feet at night. A calming ritual — not a treatment.
